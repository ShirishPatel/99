MJ Button

   A highly customizable command button. You can choose between
Standard button or Internet Explorer (IE) button style.
   Properties are included to modify:
      - Font
      - Position of the Caption and Picture inside the button
      - Distance between the borders and the Caption  (allowing you
        to save space in the Form when using many buttons)
      - Caption, Picture and Backcolor when the mouse is out of the
        button, over the button and when clicking the button
      - Colors of the borders
      - Show the Caption/Picture in greyscale when the mouse is out
        of the button
      - Style (Standard button or IE button style)
      - Play a WAV file when clicked
      - Play an AVI file when clicked
      - Etc...
   Give a professional look to your projects.
